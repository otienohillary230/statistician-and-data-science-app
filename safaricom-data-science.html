What we solve

Data science across every vertical

From fraud detection to tower placement, our models run in production across Safaricom's core business units.

🛡️

Fraud Detection

Real-time anomaly detection on M-Pesa transactions using gradient boosting and graph neural networks to flag suspicious patterns in milliseconds.

📶

Network Optimisation

Predictive models for base station load balancing, capacity planning, and QoS degradation alerts using time-series forecasting and RL agents.

🔄

Churn Prediction

Survival analysis and SHAP-driven explainability to identify at-risk customers 30 days ahead — enabling personalised retention interventions.

💡

Credit Scoring

Alternative credit scoring for the unbanked using mobile money behaviour, recharge patterns, and social graph features powering M-Shwari and Fuliza.

🗺️

Geospatial Analytics

Satellite imagery, GPS mobility data, and H3 hexagonal indexing to guide tower expansion, retail placement, and digital financial inclusion mapping.

🤖

Conversational AI

Swahili and English NLP models for Zuri chatbot — handling 2M+ customer queries per month with intent classification and dialogue management.

Methodology

Deploy

Monitor

Data drift alerts, Grafana dashboards, SHAP audits
