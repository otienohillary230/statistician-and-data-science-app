Build Roadmap

A phased build plan that gets you to revenue as fast as possible, then iterates. Don't build everything at once — phase by conversion priority.

01
Weeks 1–3 · Foundation
Core conversion loop
The minimum viable site. Home page, a single Courses page, Pricing, and Free Trial. Enough to take payments and test messaging. Everything else is iteration.
Home Pricing Free Trial One Course page
02
Weeks 4–6 · Credibility
Trust and organic growth
Add the trust layer. Blog starts building SEO from day one — compound returns over months. Case Studies close enterprise deals that Pricing alone can't.
Blog (first 5 posts) Case Studies About & Team Tools page
03
Weeks 7–10 · Scale
Segment and retain
Build out the explore layer to segment visitors better. Launch community for retention and UGC. Add Book a Demo for enterprise pipeline.
Solutions Community Book a Demo Newsletter Member Dashboard
04
Weeks 11+ · Optimise
Conversion rate optimisation
Now you have data. A/B test landing page headlines, CTAs, and pricing tiers. Build Docs centre, expand course catalog, and run community events.
A/B test Home hero Docs & Help Testimonials page Expand Blog Affiliate / referral
